Gym Management & Diet & Nutrition Management Features

Gym Management Features

Advanced Dashboard

Dashboard showcases member, membership, equipment, workout, exercise, and yoga stats, plus active membership graphs.

Memberships

Manage gym membership details for members, including specific time durations and expiration dates.

Renew Memberships

Facilitate the renewal process for existing memberships, ensuring seamless continuation of membership benefits and services.

Trainers

Trainers' profiles list assigned members, daily hours, departments, and job positions for streamlined management.

Exercises

Gym exercises feature equipment, instructions, targeted muscles, benefits, steps, and video tutorials for effective fitness routines.

Reports

Gym reports encompass membership details, exercise information, fitness reports, workout logs, and diet summaries.

Attendances

Manage employee and member attendance, track hours per day, total hours, and print attendance details in Excel.

Membership Types

Feature enables categorization and oversight of different membership plans and packages for effective management.

Auto Expiration

Automatically validate memberships based on end date, triggering expiration actions when necessary.

Workouts

Workouts include exercise details, trainer support, PDF reports, and calories burned for comprehensive fitness tracking.

Classes

Gym classes offer scheduling, attendance tracking, differentiation between free and paid sessions, and designated trainers.

Email Notifications

Email notification feature sends reminders for expiring memberships to ensure timely renewal and retention of members.

Diet & Nutrition Management Features

Advanced Dashboard

Dashboard showcases member, lead, opportunity, diet plan, and template data with monthly invoice and gender diet plan graphs.

Diet Request form Website

Customers can request diet plans on the website, selecting categories and types to tailor their preferences.

Diet Plans

Customized nutrition plans crafted to achieve distinct health objectives for individuals or groups, with printable PDF reports.

Diet Types

Varieties of dietary approaches or meal plans tailored to different health objectives, preferences, or dietary restrictions.

Reports

Various types of reports available for printing, such as Diet Plan Excel Report and Fitness PDF Report.

CRM & Leads Integration

Utilize CRM and lead information to create diet plans directly, incorporating specific dietary details seamlessly.

Members

You can create members and input their details, including gender, age, and birthdate, for diet and nutrition management.

Diet Categories

Distinct classifications for dietary plans based on specific nutritional requirements, preferences, or health goals.

Foods & Nutrients

Essential components of diets, comprising various food items and their corresponding nutritional elements for health.

Invoices

Generate invoices for diet plans, detailing costs and payment information for nutritional services.
