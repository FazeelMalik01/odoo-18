Smart Chatter UI
================

✨ Hide & Show Chatter in Any Odoo Form View

Smart Chatter UI improves productivity by letting you **hide and show chatter** 
in Sales Orders, Invoices, CRM Leads, Employees, and other form views.  
A floating **"Show Chatter"** button makes it easy to restore it anytime.

---

🚀 Key Features
---------------
- Hide chatter instantly
- Floating **"Show Chatter"** button
- Works across all Odoo models with chatter
- Clean, lightweight, and non-intrusive

---

🧑‍💼 Usage Guide
----------------
1. Open a form (e.g., Sales Order, Invoice, CRM Lead, Employee)
2. Click **"Hide"** in the chatter
3. Click the floating **"Show Chatter"** button to restore it

---

✅ Works in Odoo Community & Enterprise  
✅ Compatible with latest Odoo version
